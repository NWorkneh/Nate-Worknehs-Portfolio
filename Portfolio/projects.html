CrackerJacks

Figma

This project designed a Skip-the-Line app to solve long wait times at live events using an iterative design process. User research, interviews, and testing were conducted to build low and high fidelity prototypes. The app’s design can still be improved by working on location settings, check out methods, and typography. Challenges were faced with design consistency and team workload. Future work includes improving location information, making the app more intuitive and user-friendly, and incorporating search bars into the design. Continued testing and feedback will lead to a better user experience.

TikTok Research

User Interactions (UI)

For this project, I was tasked with studying the sharing habits of TikTok users regarding false information. The problem was tested using two frequent users and how they interact with videos, but more importantly, the news videos that come across their "For You Page" or FYP. A think-aloud observation was used, followed by a semi-structured interview. Using these two methods of user interaction research, a report was constructed that discusses and highlights the findings of the research problem.
